Frequently asked questions

We've been waiting… What took you so long?

A multitude of technical complexities and the prioritisation of family and health over work. It's taken a few years to get things back on track, and I apologise for that. The new apps have been re-written from the ground up in a different programming language to allow for cross-platform compatibility. The images are the same, but not a single line of the original code remains in the new versions.

Are you going to update your old apps?

No. These are gradually being rendered obsolete by Apple's never-ending system updates. I wanted to get these new versions made before the old ones completely disappear.

Why subscription? Your old apps only required a one-off payment.

Yes, those were the good old days 😁 Something I learned the hard way: as apps age, they require ongoing work — while sales steadily decline. This makes the business model unsustainable. When I first realised this, I resisted going subscription-only because I didn't want to inflict it on my customers. When I realised I was just delaying the inevitable, that's when I got things properly moving forward again.

What about Road Pad and Road Board?

These are currently being redeveloped and are coming soon.

What about Routes and DL25 — Mock Test?

It's on my to do list to revisit these apps and see if I can bring them more up-to-date.

The Emergency Stop section is not in the new Lessons app — why?

I'm working on it now and it's coming soon in a free update.

What about Android?

Bringing these apps to Android has been a top priority for me. I'm fighting my way through a few layers of Google bureaucracy right now, and I'll release them on Android as soon as I am able!
